Number Game

Welcome to the number guessing game!

Rules:

  • Enter a number between 1 to 9 in Easy level, 1 to 5 in Medium level, and 1 to 3 in Difficult level.
  • The computer will generate a number within the respective level's range.
  • If your number matches the computer's number, you lose a chance.
  • You start with 3 chances. If chances run out (chance = 0), you lose the game.
  • If your number does not match the computer's, the number you entered will be added to your score.
